This is where you can create an account on the Java SoC system. Just fill in the boxes and press the "Create Account" button. I suggest including your email address so that if you forget your password, I can use it to confirm your identity. One more thing, if you don't use your account for more than 90 days, it will be removed.

Note: Passwords must be 10 characters or less.
